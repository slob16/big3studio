ABOUT US

PURPOSEFUL | CURIOUS | CREATIVE | PROTECTIVE | INNOVATIVE

Big 3 is a media and entertainment company with a bold presence across the UK, US, and Canada. Built on a foundation of creative integrity and IP protection, we champion original voices and empower creators to bring visionary stories to global audiences.

We believe that great stories deserve more than just exposure, they deserve protection, strategic support, and platforms that respect artistic value. From independent auteurs to established studios, Big 3 collaborates with creators to preserve the authenticity of their work while amplifying their reach.

Our mission is to make world-class production accessible, without compromise. We are redefining the landscape of film and media by setting new standards in funding, reach, and strategic partnerships. Whether nurturing a first-time filmmaker or scaling a global release, we bring the same purpose-driven approach to every story we help shape.

At Big 3, we lead with integrity. Every decision is rooted in our commitment to upholding the highest artistic and ethical standards. We don’t just fund and produce, we protect, elevate, and future-proof content in a rapidly evolving media world.
We operate with a creative first philosophy because we know the future of entertainment depends on bold ideas, diverse perspectives, fearless storytelling and most of all authenticity.

PURPOSE | Every project begins with why. We invest in stories that resonate, challenge, and inspire.
CURIOUS | We ask better questions. We listen deeply. We explore new ground, always looking for the next frontier in content and distribution.
CREATIVE | Creativity is our compass. We champion originality and support creators in taking artistic risks.
PROTECTIVE | IP is power. We fiercely protect our creators’ rights, ensuring they retain ownership and control over their vision.
